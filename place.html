About Guatemala

Guatemala is a Central American country known for its dramatic landscape of volcanic mountains, lush rainforests, and ancient Mayan ruins. The country is often called the "Land of Eternal Spring" due to its pleasant climate year-round.

From the colonial charm of Antigua to the mystical Lake Atitlán surrounded by volcanoes, Guatemala offers breathtaking natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.
