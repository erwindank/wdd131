Color Palette

Dark theme color scheme designed for comfortable viewing and accessibility.

  • Background Primary: #0D0D0D - Deep black for main background
  • Background Secondary: #1E1E1E - Dark gray for panels and sections
  • Text Primary: #EDEDED - Off-white for main content
  • Text Secondary: #B0B0B0 - Medium gray for secondary text
  • Accent Color: #7AE3FF - Cyan blue for highlights and CTAs

Color scheme inspiration: Happy Hues and Coolors
